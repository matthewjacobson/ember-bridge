Ember Bridge

Connect your embroidery machines to the Ember web editor. A lightweight desktop bridge for seamless design transfer over WiFi.

View Manual

What It Does

Ember Bridge is a local bridge between the Ember web embroidery editor and your WiFi-enabled embroidery machine.

Design to Machine

Send your embroidery designs directly from Ember to your connected machine over your local WiFi network.

Local-Only

Everything runs locally on your computer. Your designs stay on your machine. No cloud, no delays, no tracking.

Find Machines

Automatically discovers supported embroidery machines on your network via mDNS, so setup is effortless.

Manage Designs

Browse, upload, and manage design files directly on your EmberConnect dongle or machine storage.

Monitor Progress

Check live machine status, view pending uploads, and troubleshoot issues right from the app.

Launch at Startup

Run Ember Bridge as a background service on macOS with launch-on-login and a discreet menu-bar icon.
